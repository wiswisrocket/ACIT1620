My name is Luis and I am a video editor. I specialize in social media content, event videos and YouTube videos. I can help with my experience in text animation, sound design and colour grading.

Since early childhood I have always been mesmerized with stories and films. I started making videos when I was 11 years old with stop motion LEGO movies and I knew immediately that I loved making movies. As an adult, I decided to pursue my love for making videos, telling stories and expanding my skills in it.

I have been lucky enough to be able tell so many different people's stories and their achievements and make the occasional funny video through my work with Anytime Fitness and I would love to be able to help you with your vision to entertain or tell your stories.
